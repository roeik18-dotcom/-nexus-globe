# USER-VALIDATION-SESSION.md

## Purpose

Run a user validation session consistently, without teaching, guiding, or changing the product during data collection.

> **Language is a gate for analysis, not a gate for data collection.**

---

## Before the session

- Recording enabled.
- Think-aloud encouraged.
- No explanation of the system.
- No hints.
- No UI changes during the entire validation round.

---

## Phase 1 — Language (before any click)

Ask:

> **"In one sentence, what do you think this system does?"**

Record:

- Response
- PASS / FAIL
- Annotation

Do **not** correct the participant.

Continue regardless of PASS/FAIL.

---

## Phase 2 — Behavior (observation only)

No questions.

Record:

- First click
- Time to first click
- First page opened
- Did they reach World?
- Did they identify the Primary Blocker?
- Did they identify the Next Action?
- Hesitations (>5 s)
- Wrong turns
- Moments of confusion

Observer remains silent.

---

## Phase 3 — Interpretation (after completion)

Ask:

1. What does **Explain Path** show?
2. What does **Simulation PASS** mean?
3. Why was this Provider shown?
4. What would you do next?

Record answers only.

No corrections.

## Overall Result

Select exactly one:

- ✅ Confirmed
- ❌ Falsified
- ⚠️ Inconclusive

---

## Post-session

Do **not** modify the product.

Complete all planned participants first.

Only after the entire validation round:

1. Aggregate results.
2. Compare against pre-registered hypotheses.
3. Decide whether a UX Issue or PR is justified.

---

## Important

> User feedback is evidence, not design instructions.

> A participant may reveal a problem.
> The solution is determined only after the full validation round is complete.
